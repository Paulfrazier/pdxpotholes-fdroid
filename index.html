🕳️ PDX Potholes

A personal F-Droid repository. Add it in the F-Droid app to install and auto-update the pothole reporter.

On your Android phone, with the F-Droid app installed:

  1. Tap the button below (or copy the repo URL into F-Droid → Settings → Repositories → ＋).
  2. F-Droid adds the repo with its signing fingerprint pre-filled — confirm it.
  3. Search PDX Potholes and install. Future versions show up as updates.
